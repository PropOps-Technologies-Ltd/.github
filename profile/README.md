🙋‍♀️ What is PropOps all about?

PropOps is a modern software development house dedicated to digitizing and automating the property management and field service sectors. We specialize in building high-performance, secure, and intuitive tools that bridge the gap between complex office administration and on-site contractor efficiency. Our mission is to replace legacy operational chaos with clean, automated, and scalable workflows.

📱 Product Portfolio

PropOps Native (iOS): A standalone mobile ecosystem designed for solo contractors and field service professionals. It leverages Swift, Siri Shortcuts, and Live Activities to streamline job management on the move.

PropOps Web (Core Framework): Our enterprise-grade ERP and business management framework, distributed via a modular licensing model.

Licensing Convention: Instances are identified by the client identifier followed by the "Core" suffix (e.g., DynamoCore).

Ownership Status: Licensed instances represent a limited, non-exclusive deployment of the PropOps Web framework. PropOps Technologies Ltd retains 100% ownership of all underlying source code, database architecture, and IP.
